• Do
    1. Ask for permission to visit
    2. Wash your hands
    3. Consider allergies and restrictions on decorations and gifts
    4. Turn off cell phone
    5. Keep visit short
    6. Leave if doctor or provider arrive
  • Don't
    1. Visit if you might be contagious
    2. Bring young children
    3. Bring food without checking on restrictions
    4. Cause stress
    5. Avoid visiting
    6. Smoke before or during
